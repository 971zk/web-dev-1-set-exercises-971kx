About Digi Fest

Digi Fest is an annual celebration of digital technology held in the vibrant city of Cardiff. This year, we are excited to explore the theme of ‘collaboration’. Join us as we showcase the latest collaborative projects shaping our city's digital landscape. Our program promises to be both educational and inspirational for all attendees.
