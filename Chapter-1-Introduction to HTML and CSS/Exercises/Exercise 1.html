Apple Pie

A classic apple pie recipe with a flaky crust and a delicious cinnamon-spiced apple filling.

Ingredients

  • 6 cups peeled and sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup white sugar
  • 1 tablespoon all-purpose flour
  • 1 teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • 1/4 teaspoon ground nutmeg
  • 1/4 teaspoon salt
  • 1 tablespoon lemon juice
  • 1 recipe pastry for a 9-inch double-crust pie
  • 2 tablespoons butter

Method

  1. Preheat oven to 425 degrees F (220 degrees C).
  2. In a large bowl, mix sliced apples, sugar, flour, cinnamon, nutmeg, salt, and lemon juice.
  3. Line a 9-inch pie plate with one pie crust. Pour apple mixture into the crust and dot with butter.
  4. Place the second crust on top of the filling. Seal and flute the edges, and cut slits in the crust to allow steam to escape.
  5. Place pie on a baking sheet to catch drips.
  6. Bake in the preheated oven for 45 to 50 minutes, or until crust is golden brown and filling is bubbly.
  7. Cool on a wire rack.
