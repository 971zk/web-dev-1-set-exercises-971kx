Dark Matter

Dark Matter is a science fiction television series created by Joseph Mallozzi and Paul Mullie. It originally aired on the Syfy network from June 12, 2015, to August 25, 2017. The series follows the crew of the spaceship Raza, who awaken with no memory of who they are or how they got on board.

The show has received a dedicated fan following and has been praised for its compelling characters and intricate plot.
